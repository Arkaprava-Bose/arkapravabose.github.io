Hi! Welcome to my webpage.

I am Arkaprava Bose, a fourth-year BS-MS student in Physics at IISER Kolkata. My interests lie in Statistical Physics, Statistics, Nonlinear Dynamics, Complex Systems, and Network Science. I am fascinated by how collective behavior emerges from simple interactions and enjoy studying these phenomena through mathematical modeling, computation, and dynamical systems theory.

Currently, I am exploring problems in nonlinear dynamics and complex networks, with interests ranging from synchronization and adaptive networks to dynamical processes on networks.

This website serves as a record of my academic journey, research projects, and ongoing work.
